# Move Selection Cheatsheet

Choose the smallest public move that resolves the highest-value uncertainty.

- facts weak -> `scan`
- objective, scope, terms, or constraints weak -> `clarify`
- proposal, assumption, or plan needs pressure -> `stress`
- next safe route already clear -> `handoff`

Tie-breakers:

- prefer the move that most reduces harmful uncertainty
- prefer the move that unlocks the next route fastest
- prefer the smaller public move over an internal submode description
- stop grounding once the next route is clear
